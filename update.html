Patch New Map Boss Fight

v1.2.2 — Balance & Flow SEPT 2025

Claw Flow, Titan Pain

Patch Balance

This patch focuses on tightening core combat: fluid combos for Wolverine, clearer telegraphs for Titans, and more rewarding counter windows for skilled players.

  • Increased combo buffer leniency on ground-to-air chain attacks.
  • Titan slam attacks now have clearer windup VFX and audio.
  • Reduced camera shake on repeated heavy hits for better readability.
  • Minor performance improvements on mid-spec PCs and consoles.

Faster reads, cleaner hits, sharper decisions.

Hotfix 1.2.1 AUG 2025

Princess Bar Spawn Fix & Stability

Hotfix Bug Fixes

Addressed critical issues affecting spawns and round flow in the Princess Bar map, alongside several crash fixes reported by the community.

  • Fixed rare bug causing players to spawn inside geometry near the back alley.
  • Resolved occasional crash when Titans spawned during wave 3 on low VRAM systems.
  • Improved collision on bar counter and upstairs balcony rails.

Thanks for the reports, hunters.
